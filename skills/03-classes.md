## Class Features

| Class | Class Feature | Hope Feature (3 Hope) |
|-------|--------------|----------------------|
| **Bard** | Rally: 1/session, give self+allies a Rally Die (d6 L1, d8 L5) for action/reaction/damage or clear Stress | Make a Scene: Distract target in Close range, -2 to their Difficulty |
| **Druid** | Beastform: Transform into creatures at/below tier by marking Stress. Gain creature's Evasion bonus, use specified trait. Wildtouch: harmless nature effects at will | Evolution: Transform without Stress; +1 to one trait during Beastform |
| **Guardian** | Unstoppable: 1/long rest, gain Unstoppable Die (d4, d6 at L5). Increases after dealing damage. Reduce physical damage severity by one threshold, add die to damage, immune to Restrained/Vulnerable | Frontline Tank: Clear 2 Armor Slots |
| **Ranger** | Ranger's Focus: Spend Hope + attack; on success, target = Focus (precise awareness, target marks Stress on damage, reroll Duality Dice on failed attacks vs Focus) | Hold Them Off: On weapon hit, apply same roll to 2 additional adversaries in range |
| **Rogue** | Cloaked: When Hidden, become Cloaked (enhanced stealth). Sneak Attack: bonus damage when cloaked or adjacent ally (1d6 L1, 2d6 L2-4, 3d6 L5-7, 4d6 L8-10) | Rogue's Dodge: +2 Evasion until next successful attack against you or rest |
| **Seraph** | Prayer Dice: Roll d4s = Spellcast trait at session start. Use to reduce damage, boost rolls post-roll, or generate Hope. Clear unspent at session end | Life Support: Clear a Hit Point on ally in Close range |
| **Sorcerer** | Arcane Sense: Sense magic in Close range. Minor Illusion: Spellcast Roll (10). Channel Raw Power: 1/long rest, vault a domain card for Hope = card level or boost spell damage by 2x card level | Volatile Magic: Reroll any damage dice on magic damage attacks |
| **Warrior** | Attack of Opportunity: When enemy leaves Melee, reaction roll; choose: prevent movement, deal weapon damage, or move with them. Combat Training: Weapons don't count toward burden; physical damage bonus = level | No Mercy: +1 to attack rolls until next rest |
| **Wizard** | Prestidigitation: Harmless magical effects at will. Strange Patterns: Choose number 1-12; rolling it on Duality Die = gain Hope or clear Stress (change after long rest) | Not This Time: Force adversary in Far range to reroll attack or damage |

## Subclass Details

Each subclass grants:
- **Spellcast Trait**: Which trait for spellcasting
- **Foundation Feature**: Starting subclass ability
- **Specialization Feature**: Gained on level-up
- **Mastery Feature**: Highest-level subclass ability
