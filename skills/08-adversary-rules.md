# Adversary Rules

## Stat Block Structure
Every adversary has: **Name, Tier, Type, Description**, Motives & Tactics, Difficulty (target number for PC attacks), Damage Thresholds (Major/Severe), Hit Points, Stress, Attack Modifier, Standard Attack (default damage), Experience (optional bonus activated by spending Fear), and Features.

## Feature Types

| Type | Activation |
|------|-----------|
| **Action** | Costs a spotlight/action token to use |
| **Reaction** | Triggers automatically on specified condition; no token cost |
| **Passive** | Always active; no cost |
| **Fear Feature** | Costs 1+ Fear to activate; high-impact abilities |

## 10 Adversary Types

| Type | BP Cost | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|
| Minion (group) | 1 | Easily dispatched; group size = party size |
| Social | 1 | Challenges via conversation, not combat |
| Support | 1 | Buffs allies, disrupts PCs |
| Horde | 2 | Group of identical creatures as single unit |
| Ranged | 2 | Fragile in melee, high ranged damage |
| Skulk | 2 | Ambushers and maneuverers |
| Standard | 2 | Balanced all-rounders |
| Leader | 3 | Commands/summons other adversaries |
| Bruiser | 4 | High HP, powerful attacks |
| Solo | 5 | Challenge for entire party |

## Adversary Actions on GM Spotlight
When you have the spotlight, each adversary can:
- Move within Close range + make standard attack
- Move within Close range + use an Action feature
- Clear a condition
- Sprint within Far/Very Far range
- Other fiction-appropriate actions

Spend 1 Fear to place 2 additional action tokens for extra adversary actions.

## Adversary Attacks
Adversary attacks are **player-facing**: the GM does not roll dice. Either:
- PC rolls to defend (reaction/evasion)
- GM applies damage directly on spotlight turns

Add the adversary's Attack Modifier to the attack. Spend 1 Fear to also add the adversary's Experience bonus.
