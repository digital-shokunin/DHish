# Equipment: Armor

## Armor Mechanics
- **Base Score**: Number of Armor Slots available
- **Major/Severe Thresholds**: Determine HP marked (below Major = 1 HP, Major-to-Severe = 2 HP, at/above Severe = 3 HP)
- Thresholds increase by **+1 per character level** above 1
- Mark an Armor Slot to **reduce damage severity by one threshold level**
- Slots are restored via Repair Armor downtime moves during rests
