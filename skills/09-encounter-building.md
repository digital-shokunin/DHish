# Encounter Building

## Battle Points Formula
**Starting Pool = (3 x number_of_PCs) + 2**

Spend Battle Points to add adversaries using their BP costs (see adversary types).

## Adjustments

| Modifier | Condition |
|----------|-----------|
| -1 | Easier/shorter fight |
| -2 | Using 2+ Solos |
| -2 | Adding +1d4 or static +2 damage to all adversaries |
| +1 | Using lower-tier adversary |
| +1 | Excluding Bruisers, Hordes, Leaders, or Solos |
| +2 | Harder/longer fight |

## PC Tier vs Adversary Tier

| PC Tier | Levels | Face Adversary Tier |
|---------|--------|-------------------|
| Tier 1 | 1 | Tier 0 |
| Tier 2 | 2-4 | Tier 1 |
| Tier 3 | 5-7 | Tier 2 |
| Tier 4 | 8-10 | Tier 3 |

PCs always face adversaries **one tier below** their own. Mix in lower-tier adversaries for variety (+1 BP each).

## Example
4 PCs at Tier 2: Pool = (3x4)+2 = 14 BP. Could build: 1 Bruiser (4) + 1 Leader (3) + 2 Standard (4) + 1 Support (1) + 1 Minion group (1) = 13 BP, with 1 BP remaining for adjustment.
