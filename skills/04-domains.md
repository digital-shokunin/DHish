# Domains

## 9 Domains

| Domain | Theme | Classes |
|--------|-------|---------|
| **Arcana** | Raw, volatile elemental magic | Druid, Sorcerer |
| **Blade** | Weapon mastery and martial prowess | Guardian, Warrior |
| **Bone** | Tactics, body control, prediction | Ranger, Warrior |
| **Codex** | Knowledge-based versatile magic (books, scrolls) | Bard, Wizard |
| **Grace** | Charisma, storytelling, perception manipulation | Bard, Rogue |
| **Midnight** | Shadows, stealth, secrecy, uncovering hidden things | Rogue, Sorcerer |
| **Sage** | Nature, earth power, creatures, vitality | Druid, Ranger |
| **Splendor** | Life, healing, controlling death | Seraph, Wizard |
| **Valor** | Protection, defense, formidable strength | Guardian, Seraph |

## Card Types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Ability** | Non-magical actions and features |
| **Spell** | Magical effects requiring Spellcast rolls |
| **Grimoire** | Codex-only; collections of less potent spells |

## Domain Card Mechanics

- Start with **2 domain cards** (level 1) at character creation
- **Loadout**: Max **5 active cards** at a time
- Cards beyond 5 go to the **vault** (inactive)
- **Swap during rest**: Free
- **Swap outside rest**: Mark Stress = card's **Recall Cost**
- **Level-up**: Gain new card free; may swap existing for same-level-or-lower alternatives
- Cards have: Level, Domain, Recall Cost, Title, Type, Feature text

## Spellcasting

- **Spellcast Rolls**: Use Spellcast Trait (set by subclass) + Duality Dice vs Difficulty
- Only roll when a feature requires it
- **Spellcast damage**: Roll dice = Spellcast trait value (+0 or lower = no dice)
- **Spell maintenance**: Lasts as described; GM can spend 1 Fear to end; can maintain multiple
- Spellcast rolls dealing damage count as attack rolls

## Level 1 Card Descriptions

### Arcana Level 1
- **Rune Ward** (Spell, Recall 0): Infuse trinket with protective magic. Holder spends Hope to reduce incoming damage by 1d8. On 8, ward exhausts until rest.
- **Unleash Chaos** (Spell, Recall 1): Place tokens = Spellcast trait at session start. Spellcast Roll vs target (Far); spend tokens to roll that many d10s magic damage. Mark Stress to replenish. Clear unspent at session end.
- **Wall Walk** (Spell, Recall 1): Spend Hope; touched creature climbs walls/ceilings as walking. Lasts until scene end or recast.

### Blade Level 1
- **Get Back Up** (Ability, Recall 1): On Severe damage, mark Stress to reduce severity by one threshold.
- **Not Good Enough** (Ability, Recall 1): Reroll any 1s or 2s on damage dice.
- **Whirlwind** (Ability, Recall 0): On hit at Very Close, spend Hope to attack all other adversaries in Very Close. Additional targets take half damage.

### Bone Level 1
- **Deft Maneuvers** (Ability, Recall 0): 1/rest, mark Stress to sprint within Far without Agility Roll. If ending in Melee and attacking, +1 to attack.
- **I See It Coming** (Ability, Recall 1): On ranged attack, spend Stress and roll d4 to add as Evasion bonus.
- **Untouchable** (Ability, Recall 1): Evasion bonus = half your Agility.

### Codex Level 1 (Grimoires)
- **Book of Ava** (Grimoire, Recall 2): Power Push (Spellcast, push + d10+2 magic), Tava's Armor (Hope, +1 Armor Score to ally until rest), Ice Spike (d6 physical at range).
- **Book of Illiat** (Grimoire, Recall 2): Slumber (Spellcast, unconscious until damaged or Fear spent), Arcane Barrage (1/rest, spend Hope = d6s damage), Telepathy (Hope, mental link until rest).
- **Book of Tyfar** (Grimoire, Recall 2): Wild Flame (Spellcast vs 3 targets, 2d6 magic, targets mark Stress), Magic Hand (conjure hand at Far), Mysterious Mist (Spellcast 13, fog in Very Close area).

### Grace Level 1
- **Deft Deceiver** (Ability, Recall 0): Spend Hope for advantage on deception/trick rolls.
- **Enrapture** (Spell, Recall 0): Spellcast vs target; Enraptured (focused on you). 1/rest, mark Stress to force enraptured target to mark Stress.
- **Inspirational Words** (Ability, Recall 1): Tokens = Presence after long rest. In conversation, spend per token: clear 1 Stress, recover 1 HP, or gain 1 Hope. Clear at long rest.

### Midnight Level 1
- **Pick and Pull** (Ability, Recall 0): Advantage on picking locks, disarming traps, taking items.
- **Rain of Blades** (Spell, Recall 1): Spend Hope, Spellcast vs all in Very Close; d8+2 magic. Vulnerable targets take extra 1d8.
- **Uncanny Disguise** (Spell, Recall 0): Mark Stress to disguise as any humanoid. Advantage on Presence to avoid detection. Tokens = Spellcast trait; each action spends one; disguise drops at zero.

### Sage Level 1
- **Gifted Tracker** (Ability, Recall 0): Spend Hope to ask that many questions about tracked creatures. +1 Evasion vs tracked creatures.
- **Nature's Tongue** (Ability, Recall 0): Instinct Roll (12) to converse with flora/fauna. In nature, spend Hope for +2 Spellcast.
- **Vicious Entangle** (Spell, Recall 1): Spellcast vs target (Far); 1d8+1 physical, restrain. Spend Hope to restrain second adversary in Very Close of first.

### Splendor Level 1
- **Bolt Beacon** (Spell, Recall 1): Spellcast vs target (Far); spend Hope for d8+2 magic. Target becomes Vulnerable and glows.
- **Mending Touch** (Spell, Recall 1): Spend 2 Hope to clear 1 HP or Stress (few minutes). 1/long rest, share something personal = clear 2 instead.
- **Reassurance** (Ability, Recall 0): 1/rest, after ally's action roll but before results, offer support for reroll.

### Valor Level 1
- **Bare Bones** (Ability, Recall 0): No armor: Armor Score = 3 + Strength. Thresholds: T1 9/19, T2 11/24, T3 13/31, T4 15/38.
- **Forceful Push** (Ability, Recall 0): Melee attack; on success, deal damage and push to Close. Spend Hope for +d6 damage and Vulnerable.
- **I Am Your Shield** (Ability, Recall 1): Mark Stress to redirect attack from ally (Very Close) to self. May mark any Armor Slots for redirected damage.
