# Core Mechanics

## Duality Dice System

Players roll **2d12** (one Hope die, one Fear die) + trait modifier vs Difficulty.

### Five Outcomes

| Outcome | Condition | Effect |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| **Success with Hope** | Total >= Difficulty AND Hope > Fear | Succeed. Player gains 1 Hope. |
| **Success with Fear** | Total >= Difficulty AND Fear > Hope | Succeed with cost/complication. GM gains 1 Fear. |
| **Failure with Hope** | Total < Difficulty AND Hope > Fear | Fail, minor consequence. Player gains 1 Hope. Spotlight to GM. |
| **Failure with Fear** | Total < Difficulty AND Fear > Hope | Fail, major consequence. GM gains 1 Fear. Spotlight to GM. |
| **Critical Success** | Both dice show same number | Auto-success with bonus. Player gains 1 Hope AND clears 1 Stress. |

On any failure, spotlight swings to GM for adversary actions.

## Advantage & Disadvantage

- **Advantage**: Roll additional d6, ADD to total.
- **Disadvantage**: Roll additional d6, SUBTRACT from total.
- Cancel 1-for-1. Cannot have both simultaneously.
- Multiple sources of advantage/disadvantage each add one d6.

## Difficulty Guidelines by Tier

| Tier | Levels | Standard Difficulty |
|------|--------|-------------------|
| 1 | 1 | 11 |
| 2 | 2-4 | 14 |
| 3 | 5-7 | 17 |
| 4 | 8-10 | 20 |

Adjust difficulty based on circumstance. Adversary Difficulty replaces Evasion for PC attack targets.

## Hope Economy (Player Currency)

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Starting | 2 per PC |
| Maximum | 6 per PC |
| Carry over | Yes, between sessions |
| Gained | Hope die > Fear die (+1), or Critical Success (+1) |

### Spending Hope

| Action | Cost | Effect |
|--------|------|--------|
| Help an Ally | 1 | Ally rolls d6 advantage die (only highest bonus if multiple helpers) |
| Utilize Experience | 1 per Experience | Add Experience modifier (+2 or more) to relevant roll |
| Tag Team Roll | 3 | Two PCs combine; both roll, choose one result, combine damage |
| Activate Hope Feature | Varies (often 3) | Use class/subclass Hope features |

## Fear Economy (GM Currency)

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Starting | Accumulates during play |
| Maximum | 12 |
| Carry over | Yes, between sessions |
| Gained | Fear die > Hope die on any player roll (+1) |

### Spending Fear

| Action | Cost | Effect |
|--------|------|--------|
| Extra action tokens | 1 | Place 2 additional tokens on action tracker |
| Adversary Experience bonus | 1 | Add adversary's Experience bonus to attack or raise Difficulty |
| Activate Fear Feature | 1+ | Use adversary Fear Features |
| End maintained spell | 1 | Forcibly end a PC's ongoing spell |
| Advance countdown | Varies | Tick a countdown clock forward |
| Narrative hard move | 1+ | Introduce complications, new threats, environmental hazards |
