# Combat

## Spotlight System (No Initiative)

1. **Players go first** at combat start.
2. Any player volunteers to act (no fixed order).
3. On **Fear roll or failure**, spotlight swings to GM.
4. GM takes adversary actions, then returns spotlight to players.
5. Players collaboratively decide who acts next.

**Optional Spotlight Tracker**: Each player gets ~3 tokens per scene; remove one per spotlight turn.

## Action Economy

No rigid action/bonus action/movement structure. On their spotlight turn, a PC can:
- Describe what they do narratively
- Make action rolls as needed
- Move within **Close range** free (as part of action)
- Move further requires **Agility Roll**
- Use abilities, spells, or items

## Attack Rolls

Roll 2d12 (Duality Dice) + relevant trait modifier vs target's **Evasion** (adversaries use **Difficulty**).
- Same 5 outcomes as standard action rolls (Success/Failure with Hope/Fear, Critical)
- Trait determined by weapon or spell

## Damage Rolls

- Format: **[Proficiency]d[weapon die] + [modifier]**
- Example: Proficiency 2, d8+2 weapon = 2d8+2
- **Unarmed**: [Proficiency]d4
- **Damage types**: Physical (weapons) or Magic (spells)

### Critical Damage
Normal damage + maximum possible dice result.
Example: 2d8+1 becomes 2d8+1+16 (max of 2d8 = 16).

## Multi-Target and Group Mechanics

- **Multi-target attacks**: One attack roll + one damage roll applied individually to each target
- **Multiple damage sources**: Total all simultaneous damage before comparing to thresholds
- **Group Action Roll**: Leader rolls action, allies roll reaction (no Hope/Fear generated). Leader gets +1 per successful ally, -1 per failed ally.

## Tag Team Roll (3 Hope)

- Two PCs each spend from combined Hope pool (3 total)
- Both roll separately against same target(s)
- Choose one result to apply for both
- Both roll damage and add totals together

## Range/Distance Zones

| Range | Description | Grid Equivalent |
|-------|-------------|----------------|
| **Melee** | Arm's reach | 1 square |
| **Very Close** | 5-10 feet | 3 squares |
| **Close** | 10-30 feet | 6 squares |
| **Far** | 30-100 feet | 12 squares |
| **Very Far** | 100-300 feet | 13+ squares |
| **Out of Range** | Beyond reach | -- |

Movement within Close range is free with an action. Moving further requires an Agility Roll.
