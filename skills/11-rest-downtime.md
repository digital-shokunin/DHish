# Rest & Downtime

## Short Rest (~1 hour)
- Choose **2 downtime moves** from the short rest list
- GM gains **1d4 Fear**
- Three consecutive short rests **force** the next rest to be a long rest

### Short Rest Moves
- **Tend to Wounds**: Clear 1d4+Tier HP
- **Clear Stress**: Clear 1d4+Tier Stress
- **Repair Armor**: Clear 1d4+Tier Armor Slots
- **Prepare**: Gain 1 Hope (solo) or 2 Hope (with party)

## Long Rest (several hours)
- Choose **2 downtime moves** from the long rest list
- GM gains **1d4 + number_of_PCs Fear**
- GM advances one long-term campaign countdown

### Long Rest Moves
- **Tend to All Wounds**: Clear all HP
- **Clear All Stress**: Clear all Stress
- **Repair All Armor**: Clear all Armor Slots
- **Prepare**: Gain 1 Hope (solo) or 2 Hope (with party)
- **Work on a Project**: Advance a long-term project countdown by 1

## Key Rules
- Players can swap domain cards freely during any rest
- Each PC picks their own downtime moves independently
- Resting always costs the GM Fear -- this creates tension around recovery decisions
- The long rest Fear cost scales with party size, making rest a meaningful strategic choice
